## Story

**As an** admin user,
**I want** a catalog of services/software with their costs and the ability to add manual expenses,
**So that** the profit dashboard reflects all operational costs including subscriptions and one-off expenses.

---

## Context

The profit dashboard currently shows Shopify revenue + manual expenses (dummy data). This story introduces a real expense management system: a catalog of recurring services/software in Settings, and a dedicated /expenses page for manual expense entry with filtering and aggregation. Both feed into the profit dashboard for accurate profit calculations.

**Reference:** `docs/product/mistertuga_master.md` §2.2
**Schema:** `docs/architecture/schema-blueprint.md` (Phase 2, Story 2.6)

## Dev Notes

- **No npm dependencies added.** Uses existing shadcn/ui components + Supabase client.
- **Category is optional** on manual expenses — allows "Uncategorized" entries via "Other" option.
- **Fixed monthly costs** are tracked on categories and displayed separately from manual expenses in the dashboard.
- **Aggregation** in `getExpenseSummary()` separates manual expenses (by category) from fixed monthly costs for clear profit dashboard breakdown.
- **Date filters** default to current month range for both /expenses page and dashboard card.
